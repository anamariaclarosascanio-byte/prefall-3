09   Rules — do & don’t
✓ Do
Tight headlines at –0.025em
Use negative letter-spacing on display sizes (28px+). The tighter tracking gives mass and editorial authority at large scales.
✗ Don’t
Loose headlines at 0.08em
Avoid wide tracking on large text. It looks amateurish and undermines the editorial tone of the brand.
✓ Do
Eyebrow with wide tracking
Reserve wide tracking (0.18em+) for small uppercase eyebrow labels only — it draws the eye and creates hierarchy without competing with headlines.
✗ Don’t
Wide Tracking on H3
Never apply wide tracking to heading sizes. It breaks readability and erodes the clean editorial feel Prefall targets.
✓ Do
PREFALL
Use Unbounded exclusively for the logotype. It should appear only as PREFALL — never in body copy, headings, or UI components.
✗ Don’t
Use Unbounded for nav links
Do not use Unbounded for any UI text other than the logo. It’s too wide and heavy for anything other than the brand mark.
