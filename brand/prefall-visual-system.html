Rules
  • Set in Unbounded 500. Letter-spacing 0.4em at 24pt and above.
  • Always all-caps. Never set as “Prefall” in lowercase.
  • Minimum size for print: 9pt. Minimum digital: 14px.
  • Clear space around the mark equals the cap height of the letterforms.
  • On dark backgrounds: surface white (#FEFEFD). On light: ink (#282828).

06 · Type principles & voice PREFALL
Principles

Type principles

Headlines
Figtree 400. Letter-spacing tight (-0.02em to -0.06em). Line-height 1.0–1.15.
Body
Figtree 400. Line-height 1.55–1.65. Justified with hyphens on long-form.
Eyebrows / labels
Figtree 400–500, uppercase. Letter-spacing 0.18em. 11–12pt.
Alignment
Body left-aligned (justified on long-form). Centre is reserved for the home hero and CTAs.
Quotation marks
Always curly: “ ” and ‘ ’. Never straight quotes.
Em dash
Use — (with spaces around it) for parentheticals. Never -- or - -.

Editorial voice

  • UK English spelling — analyse, behaviour, organisation, programme.
  • Industry-insider register. Assume the reader knows what CSRD, ESPR, scope 3 mean.
  • Specific over general. Use numbers, dates, named regulations, named brands.
  • No marketing fluff. Banned: transform, revolution, game-changer, leverage, disrupt.
  • Each claim earns its place with a primary source — regulation text, audited report, press release.
  • Short headlines. Long-form articles can run 1,500–3,000 words when the analysis warrants it.
